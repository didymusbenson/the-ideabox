# Pilot Episode Beat Sheet (Ch. 1)

1. Cold open: Tae-sung delivering soup to Halmoni Seo, easy warmth between them — establish the relationship before the betrayal.
2. Time-jump six weeks: the PF fund collapse notification, shown as a single devastating text message.
3. Tae-sung doing math that doesn't work, contract-end calendar on his wall with dates circled in red.
4. First tenant contact — Mi-jin asking about her renewal, Tae-sung lying smoothly, immediate self-loathing.
5. Detective Han's first appearance — a knock that isn't about him yet, but should be.
6. The Management Office's recruitment approach: a card, no name, an address.
7. End-of-chapter hook: Tae-sung walks into the first "settlement" room and the first face he sees across the table is Mi-jin's.
