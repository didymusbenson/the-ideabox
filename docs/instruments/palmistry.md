# Instrument: Palmistry (Hand Reading)

> Builds on [`../reading.md`](../reading.md). A supporting divination instrument the player can lean
> on — **active in the MVP**, not parked.

## What it is

The client's **hand** as a data source. When the player can examine it, the **lines and features**
(heart, head, life, fate lines; their length, depth, breaks) carry **fixed meanings** from the
palmistry "book." The player inspects the hand and derives facts that feed the reading.

## The book — palm meanings

Canonical significance for each line/feature (what a long vs. short vs. broken line is said to mean).
Fixed and learnable; simplified is fine.

## How it's used in the deduction

Palmistry is another **corroborating instrument.** A hand that "confirms" what the cards and the sign
suggest tightens the correct read; a hand that seems to contradict them is a signal the player has to
reconcile. Like the others, it feeds facts (surfaced as color-coded keywords) into the dialog.

## Open questions

- **Overlap with tarot** — keeping palmistry meaningfully distinct rather than redundant.
