# Method: Astrology / Star Charts

> **Status: STUB · POST-MVP (deferred).** The MVP ships Tarot only; astrology comes later. This
> placeholder captures the method's skeleton against the shared framework in
> [`../divination-methods.md`](../divination-methods.md). To be detailed like [`tarot.md`](./tarot.md)
> when this method is scheduled.

Astrology is the **"synthesis of a structured system"** method — many parts (signs, houses,
placements) that must be read *together* into a coherent whole. More combinatorial than tarot or
palmistry.

## Contract (to be filled in)

- **Subject.** A chart derived from the client — signs, houses, placements, and the relationships
  between them.
- **The book — fixed meanings.** The canonical significance of placements and their combinations. The
  standard the Score is judged against.
- **Step reveal + interpretation choices.** How the chart is read out in sequence and the interpretive
  choices made along the way — the "two valid directions" principle applies here too.
- **Aggregation into advice.** How the parts synthesize into the final outcome that feeds the client's
  story.
- **Correctness check.** How faithful chart-reading is scored for accuracy.

## Open questions specific to astrology

- **Where does the randomness live,** and how does it stay non-scripted / non-look-up-able while a
  client's chart presumably derives from stable facts (a birth date)? This tension is sharper here
  than for tarot and needs a real answer before astrology is in scope.
- **Complexity budget.** Astrology's real ruleset is large; how much of it becomes a *learnable*
  in-game book without overwhelming the player.
- **Whether it belongs in the first slice at all** — likely a later method given the above.
