# Crimson Heart — Roster & Suit Groups

**Phase:** SEED. Structure is settled; individual names and details are still open.

The magical girls are organized into teams, each themed on a **different suit
system**. Three groups exist so far, escalating from the protagonist's generation
outward to a whole new one.

---

## 1. The core four — French / international suits

**Status:** Season one. The original, now grown-up team — the protagonist's generation.

| Suit | Magical name | Civilian calling |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| ♥ Hearts | **Crimson Heart** (Bunny) | Stay-at-home mother of two (protagonist) |
| ♠ Spades | **Raven Spade** | Space researcher |
| ♦ Diamonds | **Amber Diamond** | Single mother |
| ♣ Clubs | **Onyx Club** | Nightclub dancer (alias doubles as stage name) |

These are the four who defeated the Demon Gang and moved on with their lives. See
[`premise.md`](./premise.md) for their story roles.
